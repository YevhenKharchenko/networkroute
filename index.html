About the Game

So we took regular Mahjong and thought — what if you could actually go somewhere while playing? Each completed board opens the way forward, revealing new destinations and tile layouts. We wanted to make something that just feels good to play — smooth animations, calming visuals, that satisfying 'click' when tiles match. Great for a quick coffee break or when you want to zone out for an hour.

How to Play

  • Match identical Mahjong tiles to remove them from the board

  • Only free tiles can be selected

  • Clear all tiles to complete a level

  • Unlock new trails and environments as you progress

  • Use helpful hints when puzzles get tricky

  • Same Mahjong rules you already know, but still interesting
